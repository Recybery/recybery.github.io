On the first Saturday of every month, Carrboro holds a festival in the Town Commons called the Really, Really Free Market. We are trying to show up at these events and present some of the projects that we're working on. Hopefully it will get people excited about our group and about technology in general. This weekend, we had the Arduino set up and loaded with the latest version of our morse code project. We got to have a lot of good conversations!

We needed our fliers perforated, so interested folks could tear a tab off. We repurposed a sewing machine to do it for us!
